Tanslation Process

upload

Simply upload the document(s) you want to translate from your browser (docx, pptx or xlsx format) and select your source and target language(s). Click the ‘translate’ button to start the translation and check the status on the screen.

feedback loop

Preview Lingoo’s translation and edit it directly on the screen. Save your preferred wording and Lingoo will translate accordingly next time, saving you valuable time on editing and continuously providing you with high-quality, accurate output.

download

Once your translation is done, you can download the translated file in the same format as the original file. Share your translation with your team members and other systems as necessary. That’s it, your translation is good to go!
